void SP_alarm_box ( gentity_t *  ent)

SP_alarm_box.

QUAKED alarm_box (1 0 1) START_ON You need to have an origin brush as part of this entity current alarm box model is (8 x 16 x 28) "health" defaults to 10

"noise" the sound to play over the system (this would be the siren sound)

START_ON means the button is pushed in, any dlights are cycling, and alarms are sounding

"team" key/value is valid for teamed alarm boxes teamed alarm_boxes work in tandem (switches/lights syncronize) target a box to dlights to have them activate/deactivate with the system (use a stylestring that matches the cycletime for the alarmbox sound) alarm sound locations are also placed in the dlights, so wherever you place an attached dlight, you will hear the alarm model: the model used is "models/mapobjects/electronics/alarmbox.md3" place the origin at the center of your trigger box
